FAQ

Instagram invisible characters FAQ

Can I make an Instagram username invisible?

Usually no. Usernames are stricter than bios, comments, and display names, so do not rely on hidden Unicode there.

Which character should I try in an Instagram bio?

Start with U+200B for spacing. If you need a blank looking mark, test U+3164 or U+2800.

Why did Instagram remove the blank?

The field may trim hidden characters, normalize Unicode, or block repeated invisible marks.

Can invisible characters help highlight names?

They can be tested for spacing or blank looking labels, but mark the result as pending until you save and reopen the profile.

Can I use invisible characters in Instagram stories?

You can test them in story text or labels, but stories may render hidden marks differently from bios and comments.

How do I check what I copied?

Use the homepage detector to reveal code points such as U+200B, U+200D, U+2800, and U+3164.
