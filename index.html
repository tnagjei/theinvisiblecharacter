What Are Invisible Characters?

Understanding the magic behind blank text

Unicode Magic

Invisible characters are special Unicode characters that take up zero visual space but exist as real text. They're not just empty spaces - they're legitimate characters that platforms recognize and process.

Multiple Types

We offer 12 different types of invisible characters, including zero-width spaces, non-joiners, and directional markers. Each serves unique purposes across different platforms and use cases.

Universal Compatibility
